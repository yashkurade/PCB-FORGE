CREATOR'S_THOUGHTS

PROF. YOGESH PANDIT

Project Mentor

It all started with a simple idea to help small businesses and innovators make PCBs with high precision at a low cost. The goal was clear: to create a smart, efficient, and innovative solution that gives advanced tools to independent creators and startups.

PCB F.O.R.G.E. is a remarkable achievement, addressing a key challenge in PCB manufacturing with intelligence, creativity, and dedication. By simplifying the process and making it more cost-effective, this innovation empowers creators to bring their ideas to life without financial burden or long wait times. The ability to design, test, and refine freely is a true game-changer.

A special appreciation to my students Yash, Mayur, Jordan, and Chaitanya for their dedication, creativity, and teamwork in turning this vision into reality. Their hard work has transformed an idea into a powerful solution with immense potential. Ongoing improvements will ensure the design remains user-friendly, affordable, and highly effective. This is more than just an engineering project—it is a breakthrough that could reshape the future of electronics manufacturing!

YASH KURADE

Electronics Engineer (BE) 2025 Batch, VESIT

Ever since I chose electronics as my core branch, I knew I wanted to build something that wasn’t just a “project” — but a reflection of the practical knowledge I had gained. Hardware has always been my passion, and I wanted my major project to scream that passion loud and clear. I’ve always dreamed of creating a machine with a microprocessor at its core — just like how Iron Man’s arc reactor powers his entire suit. And with PCB F.O.R.G.E., I finally brought that vision to life using the Raspberry Pi Zero W. This project was more than just circuits and code — it was a journey. Managing a team, aligning our ideas, and giving constant updates to our mentor taught me so much beyond just technical skills. But honestly, it never felt like a burden because I had the most amazing team by my side. We worked together seamlessly, turning late-night brainstorming sessions into real, working solutions. A huge part of our success also goes to our mentor, who stood by us through every high and low. He never dismissed our ideas, no matter how ambitious — instead, he helped us refine and optimize them. His guidance gave us the freedom to explore, experiment, and innovate.

I also want to express my gratitude to the workshop faculty at our college. Their hands-on support — drilling, cutting, and shaping parts exactly as we envisioned — played a crucial role in bringing our design to reality.

Looking back, PCB F.O.R.G.E. isn’t just a machine — it’s a piece of my heart, my team’s hard work, and the collective support we received. And I couldn’t be more proud.

JORDAN NEDUMPILLY

Electronics Engineer (BE) 2025 Batch, VESIT

Embarking on the PCB FORGE project—our endeavor to create an automatic soldering machine—has been a transformative journey, blending technical challenges with profound learning experiences. It began with the realization that automating the meticulous process of manual soldering could significantly enhance efficiency and precision. Our team delved into extensive research on existing soldering technologies, balancing complexity with functionality to design a machine tailored to our needs.

The construction phase tested our problem-solving skills, requiring ingenuity to procure components within budget constraints and ensure precise assembly. Programming the machine's operations was akin to composing a symphony, where timing and coordination were paramount. The prototyping phase presented challenges, but each setback prompted us to refine our approach. This journey underscored the power of collaboration, transforming challenges into opportunities and ideas into reality. In retrospect, PCB FORGE symbolizes more than an automatic soldering machine; it embodies our growth, the spirit of innovation, and the unyielding pursuit of excellence.

CHAITANYA KARANDE

I wanted to bridge the gap between professional-grade precision and accessible, cost-effective solutions. The journey wasn’t without challenges. Designing a system that ensured both accuracy and efficiency demanded relentless testing, recalibration, and optimization. What made this journey truly special was the fusion of ideas—every team member brought something unique to the table, and our mentor’s guidance helped us turn abstract concepts into tangible results. Looking back, PCB F.O.R.G.E. isn’t just a project—it’s a statement. A statement that innovation isn’t reserved for massive corporations, that with the right vision and perseverance, even students can create technology that makes a real impact. This experience didn’t just sharpen my technical skills; it reshaped my perspective on problem-solving, collaboration, and the endless possibilities of engineering. And that, to me, is what true innovation is all about.
